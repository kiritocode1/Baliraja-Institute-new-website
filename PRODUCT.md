# PRODUCT.md — Baliraja Institute Career Academy

register: brand

## Product purpose
A marketing website for Baliraja Institute Career Academy, Gangapur (Maharashtra),
a coaching institute for government-service and civil-service competitive exams
(MPSC, UPSC, Banking, SSC, Police Bharti, Talathi/ZP). The site's job is to make a
prospective aspirant (and their parents) trust the institute and send an admission
enquiry. The motto, "To Educate and To Serve", is the spine of the brand.

## Users
- **Primary:** competitive-exam aspirants, 18 to 28, often first-generation college
  students from villages and towns across Marathwada, deciding where to prepare.
  State of mind: hopeful but cautious with money and time.
- **Secondary:** parents (especially farming families) vetting the institute's
  seriousness and fee fairness.
- **Context:** mostly mobile, on patchy connections; some browse on a desktop at a
  cyber-cafe or at home in daylight.

## Brand & tone
Dignified, rooted, plain-spoken. An institution that takes itself seriously without
posturing. "Baliraja" (the farmer-king) signals service to the land and its people,
so warmth and duty matter more than slick salesmanship. Copy is direct and honest:
it admits the work is hard and the hours are long, and promises constant support.
No hype, no fabricated numbers.

## Visual register
Editorial-institutional, modelled on residential-college sites (Wesley College USYD
was the named reference): oversized display typography, generous space, a committed
color surface, real imagery, and one signature motion idea (kinetic type reveal +
a curtain overlay menu). Light/parchment ground with deep maroon feature sections.

## Anti-references (what it must NOT be)
- The default Indian coaching-class look: saffron-on-white, cluttered topper photos,
  starbursts, "100% SELECTION" banners, WhatsApp-green CTAs.
- A generic SaaS landing page (hero-metric template, identical icon-cards).
- Editorial-magazine cosplay: italic drop-caps and broadsheet grids for their own sake.

## Strategic principles
1. **Trust over hype.** Qualitative, verifiable claims; leave clearly-marked slots for
   real figures rather than inventing them.
2. **One clear action.** Every page leads to a single admission enquiry.
3. **Mobile-first dignity.** It must feel composed on a 390px phone, not just shrunk.
4. **Motion serves meaning.** Reveals draw the eye to the words that matter; nothing
   blocks reading. Reduced-motion is fully honored.
